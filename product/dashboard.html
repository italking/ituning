Manual inspection and tuning

TMaestro supports automatic tuning by default, you can manually detect and tune in the large market, click [Detect Now] TMaestro will detect the health of the cluster, if there is a problem with the health of the cluster, click [Tuning Now] TMaestro will tune the cluster.

Historical tuning analysis

In the TMaestro market, you can view the historical tuning situation, accumulate optimization CPU, Memery, and maximum optimization RT. FGC count and FGC time. At the same time, the radar chart shows the effect of tuning on each indicator.
